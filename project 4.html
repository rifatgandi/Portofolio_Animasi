Landscape Photography
Project Overview
The landscape photography project aims to capture the beauty and majesty of natural and urban environments, showcasing a range of landscapes from serene and tranquil settings to bustling cityscapes, through the use of composition, lighting, and visual storytelling techniques.
My Contributions
My contributions to the landscape photography project included scouting for locations, experimenting with different compositions and angles, capturing the unique beauty of each landscape through the use of lighting and post-processing techniques, and creating a cohesive narrative that conveyed the essence and mood of each environment.
This landscape photo captures a breathtaking view of a sunrise from atop Bukit Sikunir Dieng, with a stunning background of the majestic Mount Sindoro. The vibrant colors of the sunrise paint the sky in warm oranges, pinks, and yellows, casting a beautiful glow across the landscape.
